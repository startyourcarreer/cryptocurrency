History of BNB

Binance Coin (BNB) was created in July 2017 by Changpeng Zhao (CZ) and Yi He as part of the Binance cryptocurrency exchange. Initially, BNB was launched as an ERC-20 token on the Ethereum blockchain. The primary purpose of BNB was to offer users a way to pay for transaction fees on the Binance exchange at a discounted.
