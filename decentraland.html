History of MANA

MANA is the native cryptocurrency of Decentraland, a virtual reality platform founded by Ariel Meilich and Esteban Ordano. The project began development in 2015 with the vision of creating a decentralized virtual world where users could buy, develop, and sell virtual land. In 2017, Decentraland held an initial coin offering (ICO) that raised $26 million. This funding was crucial for the development and launch of the platform. Decentraland officially launched to the public in February 2020. The platform allows users to purchase virtual plots of land using MANA, which is an ERC-20 token on the Ethereum blockchain. These plots can be developed and monetized, creating a vibrant virtual economy.
