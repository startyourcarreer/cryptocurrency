History of TON

The Open Network (TON) was initially conceived by the team behind the popular messaging app Telegram, led by Pavel Durov. The project began in 2018 with the aim of creating a decentralized blockchain platform that could support a wide range of applications and services. Originally named the Telegram Open Network, the project was designed to integrate seamlessly with Telegram, leveraging its vast user base. The native cryptocurrency of the network was called "Gram."
